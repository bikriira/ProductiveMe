About ProductiveMe

ProductiveMe was born out of a personal struggle with time management and task organization. As a busy developer juggling multiple projects, I found myself overwhelmed and in need of a simple yet effective todo app. This inspired me to create ProductiveMe, a tool designed to help users like myself stay organized and boost productivity.

This project is part of my Portfolio Project for Alx SE, completed over a span of two weeks. You can learn more about Alx SE here.
